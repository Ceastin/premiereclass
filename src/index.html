Premier Inn Birmingham

Birmingham is a bustling city in the heart of the midlands. Formerly one of the UK's major industrial centres, Birmingham is known for its canals, great venues and for being the home of the Bullring & Grand Central shopping centre and Aston Villa FC. Our hotels are close to all the city's major attractions, Birmingham Airport and the Queen Elizabeth Hospital.
